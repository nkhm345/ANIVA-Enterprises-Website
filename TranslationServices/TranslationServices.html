About the Translator & Interpreter

Our translator/interpreter, Ms. Heju Huang, has graduate education and extensive work experience in both China and the United States. She has a Bachelor’s Degree from the Ocean University of China in Qingdao and a Master’s Degree from Georgia College & State University in Georgia, USA. She has co-authored more than six publications at various professional Journals. Being a member of The American Translators Association (ATA) and having specialized in Medical and Science Research, Ms. Huang has tremendous translation and interpretation experiences in these fields as well as other fields. For more than a decade, she has been working closely with health and medical professionals in the USA and China.

To make an immediate appointment for Chinese ↔ English Translation or Interpretation services, please contact Ms. Heju Huang via email at heju@anivaenterprises.com.
